Antoine Blondeau

Co-founer

Antoine Blondeau is a co-founder and xxx of Sentient Investment Management, as well as a co-counder and chief executive officer of its parent company, Sentient Technologies. A seasoned entrepreneur and investor, Antoine has 25 years of experience in the technology industry, having held senior leadership positions at Goos Technology, Salesforce.com and Sybase.

Antoine's caree highlights include leading Dejima as CEO, the company that powered DARPAS's foundational CALO project that later evolved into Apple's Siri. he was also chief operating officer of Zi Corporation, whose smart text input sofware was embedded in hundres of millions of devices. Antoine is an investor, board member and advisor in a number of compaines in California, Greater China and japan, and in the author or co-author or co-author of 5 patents.

He holds a MBA from the Paris Graduate School of Managegement (ESCP) and has studied at the faculties of Economics and Commerce of Chuo University in Tokyo.
